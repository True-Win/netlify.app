TrueWin Responsible Gaming

Gambling should stay a form of entertainment, not a way to make money or escape problems. Here is how to keep control at TrueWin.

Signs Worth Noticing

Asking for help may be the right step if you are spending beyond your means, hiding your gambling, borrowing to fund play, or feeling anxious about it. Noticing these signs early truly counts.

Finding Support

If you or someone close to you needs help, confidential support is available from organisations dedicated to gambling-related harm. Our support team can also guide you to the right resources whenever you ask.

Keeping Minors Out

You must be 18 or older to keep an account or play at TrueWin. We confirm the age of every customer and encourage parents to set parental controls that keep gambling content away from children.

Healthy Habits

Most players gamble for enjoyment and within their means. A handful of simple habits keep it that way, as entertainment rather than anything more.

  • Decide a budget and a time limit before you start, and stick to them
  • Never chase losses; each game outcome is independent
  • Do not gamble when stressed, upset or under the influence
  • Balance play with other activities and take regular breaks
  • Only play with money you can comfortably afford to lose
